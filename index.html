Welcome to Pelagius!

What is Pelagius?

Pelagius is an open source Discord bot written in Discord.js that was designed for a very niche but useful scenario. It is designed for use as a utility when modding Bethesda games such as The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim and Fallout 4. Many modding guides exist that users can follow to achieve a stable modded game. Pelagius acts as a source of validation for your users to help them check if they have completed your guide correctly, and troubleshoot potential problems.

What do we want from you?

Well, that depends on who you are. If you're an author of a modding guide, we'd like for you to adopt our project and use it in your Discord community. Our team is ready to help you out and troubleshoot any problems you may encounter when using or setting up Pelagius. We're also always open to ideas for future features to be added (such as support for custom files, which is currently in progress). We ask developers to test our project themselves, fork it, make their own changes, etc. Feel free to submit issues and pull requests to our GitHub repo so that they can be reviewed and considered for implementation.
